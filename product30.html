BAUER SUPREME S27 COMP GOALIE STICKS SENIOR

The Bauer Supreme S27 Senior Goalie Stick has an entry level composite goalie stick price tag with construction features found in many higher end sticks.

The paddle on the Supreme S27 goal stick is carbon composite construction with the Supreme Ergo paddle geometry for maximum rebound control.

The shaft on the S27 goalie stick has a carbon wrap to help reduce the weight and a well balanced stick. The blade has a low-density foam insert for added dampening properties. The shoulder and handle of the S27 goal stick features a griptac control zone for a slip-free grip.
