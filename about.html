About us

About Us

Shape Up Centre is focused on improving your overall fitness to bring you the best gym experience. Our mission is to inspire a healthier nation.

We render you will be in excellent health, fitness, and wellbeing. We use methods that are all-encompassing and individually tailored. We're committed to growing a thriving fitness community that supports you whatever your fitness goals.

We have created a friendly, supportive place where everybody is welcome, in a judgement free zone. We are here to help and support everybody, whoever they are. We have members from all walks of life, shapes and sizes, abilities, experience levels, and all types of goals.
We mix great music and cutting-edge science to help people fall in love with fitness. Discover what’s new in the health, fitness and wellbeing, including free workouts and nutritional hints and tips to help fast track your success.

Our trainers will introduce you to new considerations for your workout routine and introduce the proper and safe use of our gym equipment Our dedicated team ensures the best fitness experience. They show you tips for gym and fitness exercises, as well, how to choose the best diet for you.

You can choose between a range of flexible membership options to suit your needs.
We offer a wide range of classes from Cardio equipment by Strength training and Mind and Body classes.
We include it all within your membership at no extra cost to help you live the best way you can and achieve your goals.
The future of fitness is merging physical and digital. You can use our application on your smartphone, where you'll see your personally monitoring fitness progress like your daily calories burned or distance travelled.

If you're needing to refuel after class, our nutrition bar offers the finest post-workout meals, drinks and smoothies. We have dry saunas and a private locker room. Our members have free parking.
