About me

My name is Michael Szwedo and I'm an independent front-end developer, graphic designer and photographer

I am creative, visionary, and multitalented professional with outstanding ability to translate practical business requirements and objectives into creative designs, including web design, photography, computer images, graphics and software.

I've been working as an IT teacher for over 10 years before I switched to a new/old profession - Front-End Developer.

During my last time also I've made pictures for Istock by Getty Images, and graphic projects for individual clients.

Currently focused and concerned on creating websites, graphics and photography as well as a software development process. I offer high-quality solutions to fit client's needs.

I code in HTML/CSS3 and try code in jQuery and JavaScript, I create photos in my home photography studio as well as I design various graphic materials such as logos, posters, business cards, billboards, cd cover and many other. During my work I mostly rely on Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator and Adobe InDesign, which help me deliver high-quality solutions on time.

I am highly-motivated, result oriented, self-driven, hard-working, fast learner and smart eager to be a part of the team, learn new technologies, become a valuable employee of the company and bring it success. Well-organized with an ability to prioritize tasks and capability to work, develop and grow in the company. Experienced in communicating with the team and clients. Attentive and accurate to details.
