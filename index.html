About me

Hi, my name is Michael Szwedo and I'm independent web designer, front-end developer. I would like to tell you something about me and present a few of my projects. I am creative, visionary, and multitalented professional with outstanding ability to translate practical business requirements and objectives into creative designs, including web design, front-end development, edition digital images, and graphics.

I've been working as an IT teacher for over 10 years before I switched to Web design and Front-End Developing. My passion is also photography and graphic design. I've made pictures and graphics for iStock by Getty Images, as well as for individual clients.

Currently, I am focused and concerned on Design and Develop Websites, as well as a Web Application and software development process. I also create photographs and graphics. I offer high-quality solutions to fit the client's needs.

I code in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. In my work, I use the most popular tools, e.g. bootstrap, grid system and frameworks. I love to meet and learn new technologies and new trends. I am constantly improving my knowledge about UI design, coding development, programming languages, frameworks, typography, Data Bases, SQL and all that is related to Information Technology.

In my home photography studio, I've created photos, as well as I've designed various graphic materials such as logos, posters, business cards, billboards, cd covers and many others. During my design work, I mostly rely on Adobe Suite, which help me deliver high-quality solutions on time. Those work learnt me open-mindedness of the surrounding environment, and being attentive and accurate to details.

As an IT teacher, I've been learning e.g. working with prepared rough drafts of material based on an agreed brief, solving methodical problems and using of analytical skills. In my routine work I've used elements of scrum and agile and many other soft skills.

All the above mentioned skills made me being highly-motivated, result oriented, self-driven, hard-working, fast learner and smart eager to be a part of the team. I have a great willingness to learn new things and technologies, become a valuable employee of the company and bring it a success. I am well-organized with an ability to prioritize tasks and capability to work, develop and grow in the company.
I am experienced in communicating with the team and clients.

I invite you to familiarize with my web projects. The Code and technical documentation are available on my Github account.

If you have any questions do not hesitate to ask, I will be happy to answer any questions.
