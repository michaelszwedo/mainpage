About me

Hi, my name is Michael Szwedo and I'm an independent web designer, front-end developer. I am creative, visionary, and multitalented professional with outstanding ability to translate practical business requirements and objectives into creative designs, including web design, front-end develop, edition computer images and graphics.

I've been working as an IT teacher for over 10 years before I switched to a new/old profession - Web design and Front-End Develop. My passion is also photography and graphic design, so during my last time also I've made pictures for Istock by Getty Images, and graphic projects for individual clients.

Currently I am focused and concerned on design and develop websites, as well as a software development process but I also create photographies and graphics I offer high-quality solutions to fit client's needs.

I code in HTML/CSS3 and JavaScript, In my home photography studio I've created photos as well as I've designed various graphic materials such as logos, posters, business cards, billboards, cd cover and many other. During my UI Design work I mostly rely on Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator and Adobe InDesign, which help me deliver high-quality solutions on time.

I am highly-motivated, result oriented, self-driven, hard-working, fast learner and smart eager to be a part of the team, learn new technologies, become a valuable employee of the company and bring it success. I am well-organized with an ability to prioritize tasks and capability to work, develop and grow in the company. I am experienced in communicating with the team and clients. Attentive and accurate to details.
